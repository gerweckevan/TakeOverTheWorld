A native of the Rocky Mountains. Reks has become known nationwide for his artistic talents and abilities, including live paintings, airbrushing, sculpting and design! His greatest accomplishment is when he began independently began operating his own business in 1999 (T.O.T.W.). Reks now expands his art field by creating and designing illustrations for T.H.C. Living. Reks received 1st place, in a New York City professional art contest. The majestic Rocky Mountains feed for his creativity and his soul. Having grown up in the mountains, Reks has a great respect for nature and the environment. His artwork reflects the history of this land. Visions from his Cherokee ancestors guide his hand in every brush stroke. Influences from his childhood include; the outlaw Josey Wales, Star Wars, and painting with Bob Ross at age five.
